Biography

Dr. Anupom Das is a physician–researcher with experience across clinical medicine, epidemiology, disease surveillance, and health systems research.

His work spans frontline clinical care, public health emergency response, and clinical research implementation, including COVID-19 management.

He is currently pursuing a Master’s in Health Informatics at Karolinska Institutet and Stockholm University (SISGP Scholar 2025–2027).

Research interests include digital health systems, real-world data, health equity, and strengthening disease surveillance systems.
